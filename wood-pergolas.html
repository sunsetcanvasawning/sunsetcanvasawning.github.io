What Are Wood Pergolas?

A custom pergola is a great investment for any home owner looking to improve or create a relaxing outdoor living space. At Sunset Canvas & Awning, creating and designing a unique outdoor space is what we do and is what we specialize in. We create completely custom innovative pergola designs to fit your budget and outdoor needs whether it’s a free standing structure or an attachment to your existing home. We construct pergolas with the best materials available and with a keen eye on the detail of the craftsmanship. An idea of a Pergola may not be new, but how it’s designed can make it innovative with the endless possibilities for your pergola working with Greg at Sunset Canvas & Awning. Contact us today for your complimentary appointment with an onsite consultation.
