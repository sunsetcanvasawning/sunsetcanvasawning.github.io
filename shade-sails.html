What Are Shade Sails?

Shade sails and screens find constant usage among many home owners businesses. As one of the most reputable shade screen manufacturers, Sunset Canvas & Awning, consistently creates quality products for repeat clients.

Shade sails are heavy-duty fabrics that filter out certain amounts of sun, depending on the specific use of the area to be shaded. The shade sails utilized by Sunset Canvas & Awning. Are comprised of extended-life commercial-grade high-quality material. They are designed for longevity, and they resist tearing, fraying, sagging, or wearing thin.
