What Is An Arcadia Louvered Shading System?

Pergolas been around since ancient Greece and Roman times, it’s typically a wood structure that you sit under and gives you sun and eventually some slatted shade depending on the position of the sun. The Arcadia system is very unique as it has louvers that can adjust in either direction this is critical as the sun is always changing positions throughout the day. With a remote control you can change the position of the louvers a few degrees to create the perfect amount of shade. If the weather should change and we start to get a little rain you can close the louvers in the louvers will interlock creating a roof over structure and louvers will be pitched 2 degrees so it will run the rain water to an internal gutter system built into each structure. The adjustable louvered roof system is an all aluminum structure that is engineered to meet very strict California building codes.

This system can be placed virtually anywhere, we have overcome difficult placements location and meeting our customers’ requirements. Our systems are designed for residential and/or commercial applications and made to integrate with existing systems. Automatic Rain sensors which close the louvers automatically are available with these units, and also a range of accessories. There are three operating systems available, manually operated, motorized remote control, and solar powered. Please feel free to contact us for a on-site consultation.
